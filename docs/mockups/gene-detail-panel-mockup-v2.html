Protein
IncA is an inclusion membrane protein required for homotypic fusogenicity of inclusions. It contains two transmembrane domains and a cytoplasmic loop that mediates SNARE-like interactions. Deletion results in a non-fusogenic inclusion phenotype. Essential for establishing multi-lobed inclusions in co-infected cells.

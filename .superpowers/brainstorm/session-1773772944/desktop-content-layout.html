A — Three-panel: Strain sidebar + Gene list + Detail

Narrow left sidebar for strain/filter selection. Middle column is the scrollable gene list with AlphaFold thumbnails. Right panel is the full gene detail — all on one screen, nothing hidden. Most efficient for power users.

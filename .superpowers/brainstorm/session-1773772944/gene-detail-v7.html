Function
IncA is a type III secreted effector that inserts into the inclusion membrane early in infection. Contains a bilobed hydrophobic domain characteristic of Inc proteins. Mediates homotypic fusion of inclusions and is essential for inclusion expansion. Interacts with host SNARE proteins (Vamp3, Vamp7, Vamp8).
